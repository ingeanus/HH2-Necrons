\paragraph*{Resurrection Orb} \label{Resurrection Orb}

Once per battle, on your turn, the bearer can activate their Resurrection Orb. If it does, select one friendly unit with \quickref{Reanimation Protocols} within \quickref{Nodal Range}. The bearer of the Orb and the selected unit immediately \textcolor{violet}{\hyperref[Reanimation Protocols]{reassemble}} a number of wounds equal to the total number of missing wounds and number of wounds from all destroyed models.
